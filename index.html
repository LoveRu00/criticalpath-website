About

Your Trusted Logistics Partner

Incorporated on 8 April 2003, Critical Path Global Logistics has built a reputation for delivering reliable, efficient logistics solutions to industry and commerce across Zimbabwe.

Our approach is built on a two-pronged strategy. We design and implement logistics systems that leverage your existing resources for maximum efficiency. Where required, we deploy our own infrastructure and expertise to manage your entire supply chain from end to end.

From established exporters like Zimbabwe Leaf Tobacco Company to growing enterprises, our clients trust us to handle the complexities of logistics, compliance, and distribution. We remove energy-draining logistics challenges so you can concentrate on what you do best.

Services

Comprehensive Logistics Services

Integrated solutions designed to keep your operations moving efficiently

Supply Chain Management

Precision planning and execution to ensure the right goods reach the right place, at the right time, in the right quantity and quality. We coordinate every link in your supply chain for optimal performance.

Storage & Distribution

Modern warehouse complexes with railway sidings capable of handling 13 wagons per shunt and 20 trailer vehicles simultaneously. Scalable storage solutions matched to your throughput requirements.

Shipping & Forwarding

Expert handling of Reserve Bank documentation, ZIMRA compliance, and customs clearance for exports. We track your cargo from departure to final destination, ensuring smooth passage through every checkpoint.

Imports & Bond Management

Seamless movement of goods from external suppliers into Zimbabwe. We manage ZIMRA bonds including Bonded Warehouses and Transit Bonds, reducing compliance risk and administrative burden.
